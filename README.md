# Hotel-Booking-Analysis-Exploratory-Data-Analysis-EDA-Using-Python
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a hotel booking dataset to identify patterns, trends, and insights that influence booking cancellations, and pricing.
## üìä Project Overview
- **Objective:** To analyze hotel booking data and uncover factors influencing cancellations, booking trends, and seasonal patterns.  
- **Dataset:** 119,390 booking records from **City Hotel** and **Resort Hotel** between July 2015 ‚Äì August 2017.  
- **Tech Stack:** Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn  
- **Notebook:** `EDA-Project-Hotel-Booking-Analysis.ipynb`

---

## üß† Research Questions
1. What variables affect hotel reservation cancellations?  
2. How can hotels reduce their cancellation rates?  
3. How can hotels use this data to make better pricing and promotional decisions?

---

## üîç Key Insights
- **Cancellation Rate:** 37% of bookings were cancelled ‚Äî a major loss in potential revenue.  
- **Hotel Type:** City hotels received more bookings than resort hotels, possibly due to affordability.  
- **Seasonal Trends:**  
  - **January** had the highest cancellations.  
  - **June‚ÄìAugust** saw the highest bookings.  
  - Cancellations dropped significantly in **December**.  
- **Geographic Insights:**  
  - **Portugal (PRT)** accounted for ~70% of cancellations.  
  - The **UK (6%)** and **Spain (5%)** followed distantly.  
- **Price Influence:** Higher prices were correlated with higher cancellation rates.

---

## üí° Business Recommendations
- **Adjust Pricing Strategies:** Offer flexible or discounted rates during high-cancellation periods.  
- **Focus on Resort Hotels:** Provide special packages or discounts to attract more stable bookings.  
- **Target January Campaigns:** Run promotional offers during the high-cancellation month to improve occupancy.  
- **Improve Customer Retention in Portugal:** Focus on better service quality, communication, and loyalty programs for Portuguese customers.
